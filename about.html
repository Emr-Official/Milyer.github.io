About Our School

Learn about our history, values, and academic achievements

Our History

Milyer Nursery and Primary School opened its gates for operation in 2014 with 136 pupils from Baby class to P.5. Currently the school runs from Nursery to Primary Seven. This limit was strategically set to enable us lay a firm foundation for our learners on which we shall continue building with time until we complete the Primary School ladder.

Currently we have a total enrollment of 615 learners spread across board. The school is registered with the ministry of Education and sports with registration number PPS /M/243 and EMIS number 200167 for primary and 019107 for nursery.

Our Philosophy

Driven by the principle of social inclusion, our approach enables us to create a conducive environment that embraces social, religious and cultural diversity while supporting every individual learner to achieve their full potential.

We strive to offer a dynamic learning community where teachers and parents work harmoniously together in supporting pupils to become long-life learners.

Our Objectives

  1. To build a safe and secure school providing an exciting inclusive curriculum which equips children with skills for lifelong learning and achieve high standards of living.
  2. To provide equal opportunities for development for all members of the school community.
  3. To make the school a part of the community and to develop strong and productive links with community.
  4. To recognize and value parental rights to involvement in their children's education through the development of close working relationship.
  5. To prepare the children to be responsible, God-fearing citizens within the community and to give them the flexibility and skills to meet the demand of a rapidly changing society.
  6. To enable learners to establish social and personal relationship based on respect, understanding and cooperation.
  7. To contribute positively to community and national development through creation of jobs for both teaching and non-teaching personnel from within and outside the community.

Strategic Partnerships

The school management treasures the notion of partnership and team work both internally and externally. Our hands are therefore open to receive and welcome any opportunity for partnership and collaboration with all individuals, Organizations, Government that sympathize and would therefore wish to join our struggle in the pursuit of our cherished cause.

We would welcome:

  • Technical advisory support
  • Partnership on projects that benefit our learners
  • Volunteering for teaching and non-teaching services
  • Capacity building for staff
  • Donations of educational materials
  • Sponsorships for disadvantaged learners
