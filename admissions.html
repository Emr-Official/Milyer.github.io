Admissions

Join our community of learners and achievers

Admission requirements

Nursery

  • Interview fee of Ten thousand Ugandan Shillings only (10,000/=).
  • A valid birth certificate.
  • Vaccination card.
  • A pencil and rubber.

Primary

  • Interview fee of Ten thousand Ugandan Shillings only (10,000/=).
  • Report card from previous school.
  • A mathematical set for P.4 to P.5.
  • A pen, pencil and ruler.


Admission Process

We welcome applications from parents and guardians who wish to enroll their children at Milyer Nursery and Primary School. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

1

Inquiry

Check for open admission places, usually through radio adverts or from our website or you can contact the school administration to inquire about availability. You can visit the school or call us at +256773841851 / +256772646261.

2

Assessment

Your child will undergo a simple assessment to determine their current academic level. Children joining upper classes will be required to write some assessment examinations while those in lower classes will undergo oral interviews.

3

Admission

Once your child passes the assessment, you'll receive an admission letter and information about fees, uniforms, and reporting dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Children should be at least 3 years old to join our Nursery program. We have Baby Class (3 years), Middle Class (4 years), and Top Class (5 years) in our Nursery section.

Our academic year begins in February, following the Ugandan school calendar. However, we accept admissions throughout the year depending on availability.

For day scholars, school hours are from 7:30 am to 5:00 pm Monday to Friday. Boarders follow a structured schedule that includes study time and extracurricular activities.

Currently, we don't provide school transportation. Parents are responsible for their children's transportation to and from school.
