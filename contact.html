Frequently Asked Questions

The school is open from 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM Monday to Friday, and 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM on Saturdays. The administration office operates during these hours for any inquiries or assistance.

You can schedule a school tour by calling our admissions office at +256772646261 or sending an email to admissions@milyerschool.ac.ug. Tours are typically conducted on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

You can report any issues or concerns by contacting the relevant department directly, filling out the contact form on this page, or visiting the school administration office in person.

You can contact your child's teacher through the school office by calling +256773841851. Alternatively, you may send a note with your child or request a meeting during the scheduled parent-teacher meetings.
