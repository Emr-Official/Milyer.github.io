Featured Event: Annual Cultural Day

Our Annual Cultural Day is one of the most anticipated events of the school year. Students get to celebrate and showcase the rich cultural diversity of Uganda through:

  • Traditional dances and music performances
  • Display of ethnic attire and artifacts
  • Preparation and sharing of traditional foods
  • Cultural presentations and storytelling
  • Art and craft exhibitions

This year's event will be held on July 15, 2024 at the school grounds starting at 9:00 am. Parents and community members are welcome to attend.
