Our Fee Policy

At Milyer Nursery and Primary School, we strive to provide quality education at affordable rates. Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and inclusive, ensuring that children from various economic backgrounds can access quality education.

Note: All fees are paid through the "School pay" and should be done before pupils report to school.

Payment Methods and Policies

SchoolPay

Pay school fees conveniently through mobile money on any of these networks:

Airtel Money
  1. Dial: *185# or *165#
  2. Select: School fees
  3. Select: SchoolPay
  4. Follow the instructions to complete payment
MTN Mobile Money
  1. Dial: *165# or *185#
  2. Select: Payments
  3. Select: School fees
  4. Select: SchoolPay
  5. Follow the instructions to complete payment
M-Sente (Uganda Telecom)
  1. Dial: *185#
  2. Select: Bills and payments
  3. Select: Pay school fees
  4. Follow the instructions to complete payment

Note: You will receive a confirmation message once payment is successful.

Payment Policies

  • All fees should be paid before the beginning of each term.
  • A receipt will be issued for every payment made.
  • Cash payments for school fees are not allowed. It should made via SchoolPay

Frequently Asked Questions
