Welcome to Milyer Nursery and Primary School

Milyer Nursery and primary school is a mixed Day Nursery and Primary school located in Kakoge A, City West, Lira City West Division - one of the urban cities of Uganda. We are registered with the ministry of Education and sports with registration number PPS /M/243 and EMIS number 200167 for primary and 019107 for nursery.

Our school is premised on the aspiration for establishing a successful and sustainable center that provides affordable, high quality and comprehensive education services to children especially those from low income households in the community.

Academic Excellence

Consistently outstanding performance in Primary Leaving Examinations since 2016.

Holistic Development

We nurture moral, academic, and social growth in a supportive environment.

Day & Boarding

Flexible options to suit every family's needs with quality care for all students.
